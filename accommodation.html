Dickwella Beach Hotel

This seaside resort villa is located 3 km from Hiriketiya Beach and Wewrukannala Raja Maha Viharaya, a Buddhist temple, and 8 km from Hummanaya Blow Hole, a coastal rock formation that causes natural water spraying.
Furnished with canopy beds, the airy, spacious rooms come with a minibar and a flat-screen TV. They have private balconies, too. Suites include dining areas and views of the ocean.
There's a fine seafood restaurant with a deck, as well as a pizzeria and an open-air, rooftop lounge. The spa offers ayurvedic therapies, and there is an outdoor saltwater pool, a tennis court and a cricket pitch.
Dickwella Resort and Spa provides a variety of accommodation options to match your needs, whether with your significant other, friends or relatives. Offering you stunning views of the turquoise waters and easy access to the lawn, every room and suite at this Dickwella resort is filled with complete privacy and a variety of amenities.

Marino Beach Hotel

Across the street from the Laccadive Sea, this modern hotel is 6 km from the Zoological Garden of Dehiwala and 36 km from the International Airport of Bandaranaike.
Offering balconies with sea views, the down-to-earth rooms come with free Wi-Fi, smart TVs, mini-fridges and tea and coffee making facilities. Suites come with a separate living room. Room service is also open.
Breakfast and parking are free of charge. There is a rooftop terrace with sea views, a garden and an infinity pool with a children's area. Many services include hot tubs and gym facilities.
Whether it's for business or fun, make your experience truly exceptional and unforgettable by staying at the Hotel Marino Beach that offers a combination of luxury and modernity that will last forever. It has 300 spacious and luxurious spaces, built with true urban elegance.
